About Us

Drones are small aircraft devices which are camera-equipped that can capture images and videos in flight. They have already given an impact to the field of surveying and construction by giving an accurate mapping of the project and simplicity in gathering of data. Ease of work is the main objective of civil engineers on why they are and continue to use these devices. It may sound expensive, but these gadgets can really able to help.

Most of the problems on land developments and roads include time management, cost overruns, unmonitored works and slow collection of data for preliminary surveying. The time of a construction project manager is limited because its takes time to check for the whole site which also contributes to the delay of project. Delayed work will greatly affect the costs of the workers and the cost of the whole project. Instead of having a lot of inspectors, drone can easily give real-time updates with just clicks away.

The purpose of this device and program is to help the construction project manager/site engineer to save time and energy in obtaining these values that can be computed automatically. It can provide a wide variety of land-related services like determining lot areas and boundaries. The idea of having an eye on the site will make the workersâ€™ productivity high. It also aims to provide new way of collecting quantities (distances and areas).
